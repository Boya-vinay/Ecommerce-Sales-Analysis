#  E-Commerce Sales Dashboard Analysis

##  Project Overview
This project analyzes an **E-Commerce Sales Dashboard** built using Excel, focusing on **monthly performance (January–March)**.  

The objective is to extract **business insights, identify trends, and provide actionable recommendations** using key performance indicators (KPIs).

---

##  Dataset Description

The dataset is structured in a **monthly aggregated format (Pivot-style dashboard)** and includes the following key fields:

- **Month** → January, February, March  
- **Sales (₹)** → Total revenue generated per month  
- **Profit (₹)** → Net earnings after costs  
- Derived Metrics:
  - **Profit Margin (%)**
  - **Growth Rates (MoM)**
  - **Contribution %**

##  Key Insights

-  **March dominates performance**, contributing over **56% of total sales**
-  **February achieved highest profit margin (17.10%)**, indicating strong cost efficiency
-  Profit is **not directly proportional to sales**, showing variable margins
-  Sales dropped by **36.9% in February**, but profit increased → efficient operations
-  Business is **highly dependent on peak periods**, creating revenue concentration risk

---

##  Anomalies & Observations

###  February Efficiency Spike
- Sales decreased significantly, but margin increased
- Possible reasons:
  - Lower discounts
  - High-margin products
  - Reduced operational costs

###  March Sales Surge
- Sales increased by **231.2%**
- Profit increased but margin dropped compared to February
- Indicates **high-volume, lower-margin strategy**

---

##  Business Recommendations

-  Replicate **March growth strategies** (campaigns, demand drivers)
-  Focus on **high-margin products (February strategy)**
-  Balance **volume and profitability**
-  Reduce dependency on a single high-performing month
-  Implement consistent **monthly marketing strategies**

---

##  Conclusion

This analysis highlights that the business has:
-  Strong **scalability potential** (March performance)
-  High **operational efficiency** (February performance)

However, performance inconsistency remains a challenge.

>  **Final Insight:**  
> Sustainable growth can be achieved by combining  
> **February’s efficiency + March’s scale**
